9-Day Detailed Itinerary

Follow the route through Nepal/Himalayas with proper acclimatization and rest days

Day 1

Drive from Kathmandu to Syabrubesi

📏 117km ⏱️ 7 hours 📈 +70m 🏔️ 1,470m

Early morning drive through scenic countryside following the Trishuli River north. The road winds through terraced hillsides to Syabrubesi.

Scenic mountain driveViews of Ganesh HimalTrishuli River valleyTraditional villages
Day 2

Trek to Lama Hotel

📏 11km ⏱️ 6 hours 📈 +910m 🏔️ 2,380m

Begin trekking along the Langtang Khola. The trail climbs gradually through forests where you might spot red pandas, monkeys, and various bird species.

Cross Bhote Koshi RiverDense forestsWildlife possibilitiesHot springs detour option
Day 3

Trek to Langtang Village

📏 14km ⏱️ 6 hours 📈 +1050m 🏔️ 3,430m

Steady climb through forests opening to views of Langtang Lirung. The village was rebuilt after the devastating 2015 earthquake.

Views of Langtang LirungTraditional Tamang villagePrayer wheels and mani walls2015 earthquake memorial
Day 4

Trek to Kyanjin Gompa

📏 7km ⏱️ 3 hours 📈 +440m 🏔️ 3,870m

Shorter day through yak pastures to Kyanjin Gompa, spiritual center of the valley with its ancient monastery and cheese factory.

Ancient Buddhist monasteryYak cheese factoryPanoramic mountain viewsBase for day hikes
Day 5

Acclimatization and Exploration Day

📏 10km ⏱️ 5 hours 📈 +600m 🏔️ 3,870m

Day for altitude acclimatization with options to climb Kyanjin Ri or the more challenging Tserko Ri for spectacular panoramic views.

Kyanjin Ri viewpoint (4,773m)Or Tserko Ri (4,984m)360-degree mountain panoramaGlacier exploration
Day 6

Optional: Langshisa Kharka

📏 14km ⏱️ 6 hours 📈 +330m 🏔️ 3,870m

Optional day hike to Langshisa Kharka for close-up glacier views, or rest day in Kyanjin Gompa.

Langshisa GlacierYak herding groundsMoraine landscapesWildlife spotting
Day 7

Trek to Lama Hotel

📏 21km ⏱️ 7 hours 📈 -1490m 🏔️ 2,380m

Retrace your steps down the valley, enjoying different perspectives of the landscape and easier breathing at lower altitude.

Rapid descentDifferent perspective on viewsReturn to forestsEasier breathing
Day 8

Trek to Syabrubesi

📏 11km ⏱️ 5 hours 📈 -910m 🏔️ 1,470m

Final day of trekking back to Syabrubesi. Option to visit hot springs at Tatopani before reaching the village.

Complete the circuitHot springs optionFarewell dinnerPrepare for departure
Day 9

Drive to Kathmandu

📏 117km ⏱️ 7 hours 📈 -70m 🏔️ 1,400m

Drive back to Kathmandu through the scenic countryside, arriving in the afternoon with time to rest and celebrate.

Return journeyRural Nepal viewsArrival in KathmanduCelebration dinner
