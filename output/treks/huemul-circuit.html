Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about this trek.

Is the Huemul Circuit safe? +
It's one of Patagonia's most challenging treks with real risks. Proper preparation, experience, and weather windows are critical. Many experienced hikers hire guides.
What about the tyrolean traverse? +
A cable crosses a gorge that you clip into with a harness (provided). It's exciting but safe if you follow instructions. Not technical but can be intimidating.
Can I do it without camping experience? +
No. You need solid backcountry camping skills including in severe weather. This is not a trek to learn on.
What if rivers are too high? +
You may need to wait or turn back. Never risk dangerous crossings. This is why flexible schedules are important.
