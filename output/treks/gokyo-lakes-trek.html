Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about this trek.

How does this compare to EBC trek? +
Less crowded, arguably better views from Gokyo Ri, sees multiple lakes instead of base camp. Similar difficulty but different highlights.
Can I combine with EBC? +
Yes, via Cho La Pass (5,420m). Adds 3-4 days and requires good weather and fitness. Technical but rewarding.
Are the lakes always turquoise? +
Best color in autumn when skies are clear. Can appear more grey in cloudy weather. Still beautiful regardless.
