Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about this trek.

Why is the permit so expensive? +
Upper Mustang was closed until 1992 and remains restricted to preserve its unique Tibetan culture. The high permit fee limits visitors and funds conservation.
Can I trek independently? +
No, you must be with a registered guide due to restricted area regulations. This helps preserve the culture and ensures proper permits.
Is it worth the cost? +
For those interested in Tibetan culture, unique landscapes, and off-beaten paths, absolutely. It's unlike anywhere else in Nepal.
