14-Day Detailed Itinerary

Follow the route through Nepal/Himalayas with proper acclimatization and rest days

Day 1

Fly to Lukla, Trek to Phakding

📏 8km ⏱️ 3 hours 📈 -250m 🏔️ 2,610m

After an exciting mountain flight to Lukla, begin your trek with an easy walk down to Phakding. The trail descends through pine forests and small Sherpa settlements, crossing several suspension bridges adorned with prayer flags.

Thrilling flight to Lukla airportFirst views of Kusum KanguruCross suspension bridges over Dudh KoshiPrayer wheels and mani stones
Day 2

Trek to Namche Bazaar

📏 11km ⏱️ 6 hours 📈 +830m 🏔️ 3,440m

Today's trek involves a steady climb to Namche Bazaar. After entering Sagarmatha National Park at Monjo, the trail climbs steeply through pine forests. If the weather is clear, catch your first glimpse of Everest!

First view of Everest (if clear)Hillary Suspension BridgeEnter Sagarmatha National ParkNamche Bazaar - Sherpa capital
Day 3

Acclimatization Day in Namche

📏 4km ⏱️ 3 hours 📈 +400m 🏔️ 3,440m

Essential acclimatization day. Hike to the Everest View Hotel for panoramic mountain views, visit the Sherpa Cultural Museum, or explore the bustling market streets of Namche.

Hike to Everest View HotelVisit Sherpa MuseumExplore Namche marketViews of Everest, Lhotse, Ama Dablam
Day 4

Trek to Tengboche

📏 10km ⏱️ 5 hours 📈 +420m 🏔️ 3,860m

Contour along the hillside with magnificent views of Everest, Nuptse, Lhotse, and Ama Dablam. After crossing the Dudh Koshi, climb through rhododendron forests to Tengboche Monastery.

Tengboche MonasterySpectacular Ama Dablam viewsRhododendron forests (spring)Possible wildlife sightings
Day 5

Trek to Dingboche

📏 11km ⏱️ 5 hours 📈 +550m 🏔️ 4,410m

Descend through forests to Deboche, cross the Imja Khola, and climb gradually to the village of Dingboche. The landscape becomes more barren as you gain altitude.

Cross Imja KholaViews of Island PeakTraditional stone-walled fieldsStunning mountain panoramas
Day 6

Acclimatization Day in Dingboche

📏 5km ⏱️ 3 hours 📈 +400m 🏔️ 4,410m

Another crucial acclimatization day. Hike to Nagarjun Hill or Chukhung Valley for spectacular views and better altitude adaptation.

Hike to Nagarjun Hill (5100m)360-degree mountain viewsSpot climbing routes on Ama DablamRest and prepare for higher altitudes
Day 7

Trek to Lobuche

📏 11km ⏱️ 5 hours 📈 +530m 🏔️ 4,940m

Trek through the terminal moraine of the Khumbu Glacier. After a steep climb to Thukla Pass, visit memorials dedicated to climbers who lost their lives on Everest.

Thukla Pass memorial areaViews of Pumori and NuptseAlpine desert landscapeMemorial stupas for climbers
Day 8

Trek to Gorak Shep, Visit Everest Base Camp

📏 15km ⏱️ 8 hours 📈 +224m 🏔️ 5,164m

The big day! Trek to Gorak Shep for lunch, then continue to Everest Base Camp. Celebrate at 5,364m before returning to Gorak Shep for the night.

Everest Base Camp achievementKhumbu Icefall viewsGlacier walking experiencePrayer flags at base camp
Day 9

Hike to Kala Patthar, Descend to Pheriche

📏 13km ⏱️ 7 hours 📈 +386m 🏔️ 4,280m

Early morning climb to Kala Patthar (5,545m) for sunrise over Everest - the best views of the entire trek. Descend all the way to Pheriche.

Sunrise from Kala PattharBest views of Everest summit360-degree Himalayan panoramaRapid descent for recovery
Day 10

Trek to Namche Bazaar

📏 20km ⏱️ 7 hours 📈 -840m 🏔️ 3,440m

Long but easier day descending through Tengboche and Phunki Thenga back to Namche Bazaar. Enjoy the thicker air and celebrate your achievement!

Rapid descent through villagesReturn to thicker airCelebrate in NamcheHot shower and good food
Day 11

Trek to Lukla

📏 19km ⏱️ 7 hours 📈 -580m 🏔️ 2,860m

Final trekking day! Descend through Monjo and Phakding back to Lukla. Traditional celebration with your trekking crew in the evening.

Final mountain viewsCelebrate with trekking crewTraditional farewell partyPrepare for flight out
Day 12

Fly to Kathmandu

📏 0km ⏱️ 0.5 hours 📈 -1460m 🏔️ 1,400m

Weather permitting, take an early morning flight back to Kathmandu. Rest of the day free to relax, shop for souvenirs, or explore the city.

Scenic mountain flightReturn to civilizationHot shower and city comfortsCelebration dinner in Thamel

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about this trek.

Do I need previous trekking experience? +
No, but good physical fitness is essential. The trek is non-technical but demanding due to altitude and duration.
Can I trek independently? +
Yes, it's allowed, but hiring a guide is highly recommended for safety, navigation, and cultural insights.
What about altitude sickness? +
It's a real risk above 3,000m. Follow the itinerary's acclimatization days, stay hydrated, and descend if symptoms worsen.
Is travel insurance mandatory? +
While not legally required, it's essential. Ensure it covers trekking up to 6,000m and helicopter evacuation.
What if I can't get a flight to/from Lukla? +
Weather delays are common. Budget 2-3 buffer days. Helicopter shuttles or the overland route via Salleri are alternatives.
