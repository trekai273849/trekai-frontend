Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about this trek.

How does this compare to the Inca Trail? +
More focused on mountain scenery than culture. Higher altitude, more remote, arguably more spectacular peaks. No permits needed, less crowded.
Can I do it independently? +
Yes, but most hire arrieros (muleteers) for gear transport. Solo trekkers should be experienced with navigation and altitude.
Is acclimatization really necessary? +
Absolutely. The pass is nearly 4,800m. Spend at least 2-3 days in Huaraz first, ideally doing day hikes like Laguna 69.
