Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about this trek.

Is it really that difficult? +
Yes. The GR20 is extremely challenging with technical scrambling, long days, and basic facilities. Many underestimate it and quit. Proper fitness and experience essential.
North to south or south to north? +
North to south is traditional and recommended. The north is harder, so you tackle it while fresh. South has easier escape options if needed.
Refuges or camping? +
Refuges book up but guarantee a bed and meals. Camping is cheaper and always available but means carrying more gear on tough terrain.
