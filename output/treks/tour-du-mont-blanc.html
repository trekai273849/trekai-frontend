Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about this trek.

Do I need to book refuges in advance? +
Yes, absolutely essential in July-August. Book 2-4 months ahead. June and September are easier but still recommended to book ahead.
Can I wild camp? +
Wild camping is generally prohibited except at high altitude (above 2500m) and only sunset to sunrise. Most hikers use the excellent refuge system.
Which direction should I go? +
Most go counter-clockwise for gentler start and to get major climbs done while fresh. Both directions have merits.
Can I do it without a guide? +
Yes, the trail is well-marked and most hikers go independently. Maps and basic navigation skills are sufficient.
What about language barriers? +
English is widely spoken in tourist areas. Basic French phrases are appreciated and helpful in remote areas.
