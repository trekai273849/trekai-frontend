Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about this trek.

When can I see the midnight sun? +
Late May to mid-July. The sun never sets, creating surreal 24-hour daylight. Best experienced from mountain summits.
Is wild camping really allowed? +
Yes! Norway's 'allemannsretten' (right to roam) allows wild camping. Stay 150m from houses, leave no trace.
How difficult are the hikes? +
Varied. Some peaks require scrambling and exposure comfort. Many easier coastal walks available. Weather is main challenge.
Do I need a car? +
Not essential but recommended. Buses exist but limited. Car allows flexibility and access to trailheads.
