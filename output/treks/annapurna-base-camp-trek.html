Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about this trek.

ABC or EBC - which is better? +
ABC is shorter, lower altitude, more varied scenery (forests to alpine), and reaches a dramatic amphitheater. EBC is longer, higher, more challenging, with Sherpa culture. Both are incredible.
Can I do it independently? +
Yes, the trail is well-marked and teahouses are frequent. Many solo trekkers. A guide adds cultural insights and safety.
How cold is base camp? +
Can be -10¬∞C at night in autumn/spring, colder in winter. Teahouses have blankets but bring a good sleeping bag.
Is altitude a problem? +
At 4,130m, some people feel mild symptoms. The itinerary allows gradual acclimatization. Going slower helps.
