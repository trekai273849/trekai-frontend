üåü Why This Trek?

The Everest Base Camp trek is more than just a walk to the foot of the world's highest mountain. It's a journey through the heart of Sherpa culture, past ancient Buddhist monasteries, and through some of the most spectacular mountain scenery on Earth. You'll trek through the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Sagarmatha National Park, cross high suspension bridges adorned with prayer flags, and experience the legendary hospitality of the Sherpa people.
