About Smart Trails

Smart Trails was founded with a mission to empower hikers and adventurers through personalized, AI-powered trekking itineraries. Whether you're looking to explore the Alps, Himalayas, Andes or other stunning destinations, our platform generates custom treks tailored to your preferences.

We understand that planning outdoor adventures can be overwhelming. That's why Smart Trails uses AI to optimize your itinerary for weather, terrain, distance, and experience level. We offer features like offline maps, packing checklists, and weather-aware route planning to ensure every trekker feels confident, prepared, and inspired.

Join us as we revolutionize trekking for the modern adventurer.
