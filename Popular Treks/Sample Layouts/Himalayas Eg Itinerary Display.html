About This Trek

The Everest Base Camp trek is more than just a walk to the foot of the world's highest mountain. It's a journey through the heart of Sherpa culture, past ancient Buddhist monasteries, and through some of the most spectacular mountain scenery on Earth. You'll trek through the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Sagarmatha National Park, cross high suspension bridges adorned with prayer flags, and experience the legendary hospitality of the Sherpa people.

🏔️ Culture & Safety

Cultural Etiquette

  • • Always walk clockwise around mani stones and stupas
  • • Remove shoes before entering monasteries
  • • Don't touch religious artifacts or paintings
  • • Ask permission before photographing people
  • • Use right hand for giving and receiving

Altitude Sickness Prevention

  • ⚠️ Ascend slowly, no more than 300-500m per day above 3000m
  • ⚠️ Stay hydrated, drink 3-4 liters daily
  • ⚠️ Avoid alcohol and sleeping pills
  • ⚠️ Follow acclimatization days in itinerary
